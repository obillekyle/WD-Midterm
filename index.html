TWICE 3rd Japanese Album "Perfect World" is Out Now!

July 28, 2021

TWICE, one of the biggest girl groups in K-pop, solidified their dominance in the Japanese music scene with the release of Perfect World, their third full-length Japanese studio album. Released on July 28, 2021, the album showcases a more refined, mature, and powerful side of TWICE, departing from their usual bright and bubbly concepts. Instead, Perfect World embraces bold confidence, emotional depth, and a mix of dynamic genres, making it one of TWICE's most sonically diverse Japanese albums.

Perfect World was a major success in Japan, topping several charts and further establishing TWICE's presence in the Japanese music industry. The album debuted at No. 1 on the Oricon Albums Chart and performed well on Billboard Japan. Fans and critics praised TWICE for their vocal growth, bold concept, and diverse musical styles.
